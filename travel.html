My Travel Blog

This travel blog presents the places I have lived in and visited in chronological order, along with brief personal experiences from each place.

Kerala – Birthplace (Malappuram)

Kerala is my birthplace, and I am from Malappuram district. The place is known for its greenery, tropical climate, and heavy monsoon seasons. The people are friendly and community-oriented. Malappuram has a strong football culture, with widespread interest in the sport across all age groups. The food culture, especially Malabar cuisine, including biryani and traditional dishes, reflects the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Andhra Pradesh – Long-Term Stay

I lived in Andhra Pradesh for eight years due to my father’s work. Staying for a long period helped me understand the local lifestyle, language, traditions, and culture. This experience improved my adaptability and gave me exposure to a different regional environment within India.
